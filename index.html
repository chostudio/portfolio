About Me

Hi! I enjoy creating interactive experiences, whether that's an app, game, website, or even exploring a new medium. My speciality is combining design and functionality to produce meaningful results for all users.

I love learning and trying new things, in terms of coding and more! I taught myself how to write ambidexterously. I like to 3D model. I find typography fascinating.
